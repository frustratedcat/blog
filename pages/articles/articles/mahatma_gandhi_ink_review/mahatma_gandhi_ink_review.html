Mahatma Ghandi

An ink from De Atramentis

The Basics

I purchased Mahatma Gandhi from The Goulet Pen Company, and used it during December 2024 with a TWSBI ECO Fountain Pen - Dark Sapphire.

The Color

The ink is a dull, dreadful orange color that I just can't stand the sight of. The writing experience is a bit scratchy rather and not as smooth as the other De Artramentis ink I used Document Fuchsia. Personally, I don't mind a less smooth ink, but many people won't like this ink due to that.
